# Business Model Canvas for SQL Query Automator

## Customer Segments
- Data engineers and analytics teams in mid to large enterprises.
- Companies utilizing cloud-based data warehouses (e.g., AWS Redshift, Google BigQuery).
- Organizations with high data query demands and limited engineering resources.

## Value Propositions
- AI-driven automation of ad-hoc SQL queries, reducing manual effort by up to 70%.
- Increased productivity for data teams, allowing them to focus on strategic tasks rather than repetitive queries.
- User-friendly interface that simplifies query generation for non-technical users.
- Integration capabilities with existing data platforms and BI tools.

## Channels
- Direct sales through the Axentx website and dedicated sales team.
- Partnerships with cloud service providers for bundled offerings (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud).
- Online marketing through targeted ads and content marketing focusing on data engineering pain points.
- Webinars and workshops to demonstrate product capabilities and gather leads.

## Customer Relationships
- Dedicated account management for enterprise clients.
- Online support via chat and email, with a knowledge base for self-service.
- Community forums for user engagement and feedback.
- Regular updates and newsletters to keep customers informed about new features and best practices.

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription-based pricing model: 
  - Tier 1: $49/month (1 user) / 1,600 THB
  - Tier 2: $149/month (5 users) / 4,800 THB
  - Tier 3: $499/month (20 users) / 16,000 THB
- One-time setup fee for enterprise clients: $2,000 / 64,000 THB.
- Potential revenue from premium features or add-ons.

## Key Resources
- Development team skilled in AI and SQL technologies.
- Cloud infrastructure for hosting the application and managing data.
- Marketing and sales team for customer acquisition and support.
- Intellectual property related to AI algorithms for query generation.

## Key Activities
- Continuous development and improvement of the SQL query automation tool.
- Marketing campaigns to raise awareness and generate leads.
- Customer support and onboarding to ensure user satisfaction and retention.
- Data analysis to refine AI models and improve query accuracy.

## Key Partners
- Cloud service providers (AWS, Google Cloud, Azure) for integration and co-marketing.
- Data analytics and BI tool vendors for partnership opportunities.
- Industry influencers and thought leaders for endorsements and visibility.
- Educational institutions for training programs and workshops.

## Cost Structure
- Salaries for development, marketing, and support teams.
- Cloud hosting and infrastructure costs (estimated at $500/month / 16,000 THB).
- Marketing and advertising expenses (estimated at $2,000/month / 64,000 THB).
- Customer support tools and software subscriptions (estimated at $300/month / 9,600 THB).
