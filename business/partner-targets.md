# Partner Targets
## Introduction
The sql-query-automator aims to simplify the workflow of data engineers and analytics teams by automating SQL queries. To enhance its functionality and reach a broader audience, strategic partnerships with complementary SaaS/API services are crucial. This document outlines a partner integration roadmap, highlighting 7 key targets for integration.

## Partner Integration Roadmap

1. **Tableau**
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Tableau Public offers limited data storage and row limits.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Enhances data visualization capabilities for users, solving the job of data analysts and scientists who need to present complex data insights.
   - **Affiliate/Revenue-Share**: Tableau has a partner program that offers revenue sharing opportunities.

2. **Google BigQuery**
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: 1 TB of querying data per month, with limited rows for free.
   - **Integration Effort**: Large (L)
   - **Value-Add**: Provides a powerful backend for data warehousing and analytics, solving the job of data engineers who need scalable data storage and querying capabilities.
   - **Affiliate/Revenue-Share**: Google Cloud Partner Advantage offers revenue sharing and incentives.

3. **AWS Redshift**
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Offers a free tier with limited storage and querying capabilities.
   - **Integration Effort**: Large (L)
   - **Value-Add**: Offers a fully managed data warehouse service, solving the job of data engineers and architects who need to manage large datasets.
   - **Affiliate/Revenue-Share**: AWS has a partner network with potential for revenue sharing.

4. **Microsoft Power BI**
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Limited to 1 GB of storage and restricted publishing capabilities.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Enhances business analytics capabilities, solving the job of business analysts and decision-makers who need data-driven insights.
   - **Affiliate/Revenue-Share**: Microsoft Partner Program offers incentives and revenue sharing opportunities.

5. **Snowflake**
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Offers a free trial with limited credits.
   - **Integration Effort**: Large (L)
   - **Value-Add**: Provides cloud-based data warehousing, solving the job of data engineers and architects who need scalable, secure data storage and analytics.
   - **Affiliate/Revenue-Share**: Snowflake Partner Network offers revenue sharing and incentives.

6. **Domo**
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Limited to a single user with restricted features.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Offers a cloud-based platform for data integration and analytics, solving the job of business users who need real-time data insights.
   - **Affiliate/Revenue-Share**: Domo Partner Connect offers revenue sharing opportunities.

7. **Airtable**
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Limited to 1,200 records per base with restricted features.
   - **Integration Effort**: Small (S)
   - **Value-Add**: Enhances data management and collaboration capabilities, solving the job of teams who need to manage and analyze data in a flexible, scalable way.
   - **Affiliate/Revenue-Share**: Airtable Partner Program offers incentives and potential for revenue sharing.

## Prioritization
The prioritization of these integrations is based on the potential value-add to the sql-query-automator's users, the integration effort required, and the availability of affiliate or revenue-share programs. Partners with large integration efforts but significant value-add and revenue-sharing potential, like Google BigQuery and Snowflake, are prioritized due to their impact on enhancing the product's capabilities and revenue potential.
