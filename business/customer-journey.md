# Customer Journey Map for SQL Query Automator

## Phase 1: Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Data engineers and analytics teams experience bottlenecks due to repetitive SQL query tasks.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about automation tools; existing tools are complex or not tailored for ad-hoc queries.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration and overwhelm due to time-consuming manual queries.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content showcasing the benefits of automation; webinars demonstrating ease of use.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and engagement with educational content (e.g., downloads, video views).

---

## Phase 2: Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Users actively search for solutions to streamline SQL query processes.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelmed by options; skepticism about the effectiveness of AI in generating queries.
- **User Emotions**: Hopeful yet cautious; desire for a solution but wary of potential complexity.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Free trials or demos; customer testimonials and case studies highlighting successful implementations.
- **Metric**: Number of demo requests and trial sign-ups.

---

## Phase 3: Try
- **Trigger Event**: Users begin using the SQL Query Automator tool during the trial phase.
- **Friction Points**: Initial learning curve; potential integration issues with existing systems.
- **User Emotions**: Curiosity mixed with anxiety; excitement about potential time savings but concern over usability.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Onboarding support, tutorials, and responsive customer service to assist with initial setup.
- **Metric**: Trial usage metrics (e.g., number of queries generated, time saved).

---

## Phase 4: Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Users decide to fully implement the SQL Query Automator after a successful trial.
- **Friction Points**: Resistance to change from traditional methods; potential pushback from team members.
- **User Emotions**: Confidence in the tool's capabilities; satisfaction from improved efficiency but apprehension about team buy-in.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Success stories from similar teams; ongoing support and training sessions to ensure smooth transition.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from trial to paid subscriptions.

---

## Phase 5: Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Users explore additional features or integrations after successful adoption.
- **Friction Points**: Limited awareness of advanced features; potential budget constraints for additional licenses.
- **User Emotions**: Enthusiasm for further optimization; concern about costs versus benefits.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Personalized recommendations for feature usage; loyalty discounts or bundled offers for additional licenses.
- **Metric**: Rate of feature adoption and upsell conversions.
