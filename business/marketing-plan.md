## Positioning
**Primary Positioning Statement:**  
"Automate your SQL queries effortlessly with our AI-driven SQL Query Automator, empowering data engineers to focus on strategic insights instead of repetitive tasks."

**Alternative Positioning Statements:**  
1. "Transform your data workflow with AI-powered SQL automation, enabling analytics teams to generate queries in seconds."
2. "Unlock the full potential of your data with our SQL Query Automator, designed to save time and enhance productivity for data engineers."
3. "Revolutionize your data querying process with our intelligent SQL automation tool, freeing your team to drive impactful decisions."

1. **Persona 1: Data Engineer - Sarah Thompson**  
   - **Daily Job:** Building and optimizing data pipelines, writing complex SQL queries for data extraction and transformation.  
   - **Budget:** $10,000/year for tools and automation solutions.

2. **Persona 2: Analytics Manager - John Martinez**  
   - **Daily Job:** Overseeing analytics projects, collaborating with data engineers, and generating reports for stakeholders.  
   - **Budget:** $15,000/year for analytics tools and data management solutions.

3. **Persona 3: Business Intelligence Analyst - Emily Chen**  
   - **Daily Job:** Analyzing data trends, creating dashboards, and performing ad-hoc queries to support business decisions.  
   - **Budget:** $8,000/year for BI tools and query automation solutions.
